Product Overview

Eido is a cloud-based Intune management and monitoring tool designed to streamline and enhance device management for Enterprises, SMBs, and MSPs. Built to extend Microsoft Intune's capabilities, Eido provides the deeper insights and advanced functionality that IT teams need to effectively manage modern device environments.

Unlike basic Intune management, Eido delivers comprehensive patch monitoring, application metering, policy management, and enterprise integrations in one unified platform. Whether you're managing 500 devices or 50,000, Eido scales with your needs while providing the granular control and visibility that native Intune lacks.

          Frequently Asked Questions

          Common questions about Eido and Intune management:

          Can Eido develop custom integrations for my needs?

          +

          Yes, if you're using a service desk or tool we currently don't support, our development team can create custom integrations utilizing available APIs to cater to your unique requirements.

          Is there a minimum commitment or contract?

          +

          No, Eido operates on a flexible per-device cost model with decreasing costs as volume increases. No pressure of long-term commitments or contracts required.

          What Intune metrics can I alert on?

          +

          Real-time alerts for configuration policy issues, application installation failures, and compliance problems - going beyond the compliance-only alerts in native Intune.

          Can I invite my customers to Eido?

          +

          Yes, Eido's multi-tenant model allows customer invitations to view their Intune tenants while you maintain overarching view. Customers can create alerts, dashboards, and get complete device visibility.

          How does Eido's patch monitoring work?

          +

          Eido provides agentless update tracking at the patch level for Windows, Mac, and iOS devices, ensuring comprehensive security oversight without additional software deployment.

          What warranty information does Eido track?

          +

          Instant access to warranty details for Dell, HP, and Lenovo devices directly within Eido dashboards and reports, providing complete asset lifecycle management.
